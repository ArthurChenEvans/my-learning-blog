Who am I? What is this site?

This site tracks my web development learning journey and helps me reinforce concepts by teaching them in my own words.

Just so this is clear: from the data structure page onwards, I have used AI to generate example code (I'm lazy, I know), however, I have ensured to do a free recall where I teach everything and test it in an application before I ask the generation. I only ask for a generation once I have gotten 100% of the information correct. Of course, I also gloss over the code to ensure it is correct.

Additionally, anything noted with a '*' right after it means I will give a definition for it at the bottom of the page.
